Resources

While we process your complaint, you may find some of these resources helpful.

Legal advice and representation in civil cases for low-income Utahns
Utah Legal Services is a nonprofit law office that provides free legal help to low-income Utahns in non-criminal cases. Their website also has more information for consumers.
Salt Lake City Office Phone Number: (801) 328-8891

The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)
The CFPB is a U.S. federal government agency that enforces consumer protection laws, educates people about financial topics, and takes consumer complaints. You can submit your complaint to the CFPB, too, and find answers to hundreds of financial questions on their website.
CFPB Phone Number: (855) 411-2372

The Federal Communications Commission (FCC)
The FCC is a U.S. federal government agency that regulates television, radio, satellite, and cable communications in the U.S. If your complaint is about unwanted calls or telecom billing and services, you can also file it with the FCC.
FCC Phone Number: 888-CALL FCC (225-5322)


Various businesses and professionals are required to license and/or register with the Utah Department of Commerce. You can check this list to see which division of the Department a certain type of business or professional is supposed to license and/or register with.

You can also directly search for a business or professional you’ve interacted with by using one of these tools:

Search for registered entities with the Division of Consumer Protection
Most businesses must register with the state Division of Consumer Protection. If you believe an individual or group may have pretended to be a real business when they are not, you can use this search to see if they are registered.
Division of Consumer Protection Phone Number: (800) 721-7233

Search for licensees with the Division of Securities
Individuals and companies that do securities business in Utah must have a license. This website will let you see if a business is licensed.
Division of Securities Phone Number: (801) 530-6600

Search for licensees with the Division of Real Estate
Appraisal agents, real estate agents, and real estate companies must be licensed and registered with the state. You can use this search to see if an agent or company is licensed.
Division of Real Estate Phone Number: (801) 530-6747

Search for licensees with the Division of Occupational and Professional Licensing (DOPL)
DOPL licenses over 60 types of professionals, such as nurses, barbers, or plumbers. If someone has been licensed by DOPL, this means they meet some minimum standard for their profession. This search will let you see if a professional you’ve interacted with is licensed.
DOPL Phone Number: (801) 530-6628

Search for actions taken by the Division of Occupational and Professional Licensing (DOPL)
DOPL investigates and disciplines professionals who have committed unlawful or unethical acts. You can use this search to see if a certain professional has been disciplined by DOPL in the past.
DOPL Phone Number: (801) 530-6628
